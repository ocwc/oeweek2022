All open educators, students, policymakers, technologists and enthusiasts, wherever you may be, are invited to celebrate their Open Education work by hosting events and activities and sharing their open assets during OE Week 2023.

Open Education Activities could be events or sessions related to the intersection of the Open movement with Education that take place during the Open Education Week period. These activities can be live and in-person, online or asynchronous. They can be a series or one, although we encourage you to plan more than webinars and online live presentations. Try something different and creative to drive participation and promote collaboration and networking across OE Week. Use the OEWeek Promotional Materials for your activity.
