Contribute to Open Education Week

Every year since 2012 institutions, organizations, and individuals around the world spend one week in March sharing, discussing and celebrating their contributions to the ideals of Open Education.

All open educators, students, policymakers, technologists and enthusiasts, wherever you may be, are invited to host events and activities or share or contribute a resource or asset to OE Week 2023.

Contributions are divided in two main categories: Activities and Assets.

Contribute activity           Contribute asset

Activities are events or sessions that take place within a specific time frame. These can either be live in-person local, online or asynchronous activities. They can be a series or one, although we encourage contributions that extend beyond webinars and online live presentations. Try something different and creative to drive participation and promote collaboration and networking across OE Week.

Assets are the tangible resources, repositories or technology that are produced for use through open education initiatives. Add your asset to the OE Week Library to share and highlight your favorite resources.

Please note:

  • All entries must be openly licensed. (Learn about how to use CC licenses.)
  • Once you share your OEWeek contribution you will receive an email.
  • Once your contribution is approved you will receive an email confirming that your entry will be displayed on the OE Week website.
  • The data collected by the OE Global through this form is specifically used for OE Week operational and communications purposes. The email address you include will be added to an OEGlobal list for future notifications about OE events and opportunities.
